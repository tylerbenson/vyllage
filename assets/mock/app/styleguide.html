Buttons

Buttons can be a button element or anything with a .button class. I leaned towards semantic class naming. Feel free to mix and match the patterns.

Adding icons is simple. Just use an i tag with the corresponding Ionicon class name.

Lastly, all styles can be found at styles/components/_buttons.scss
